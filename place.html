About

Mount Fuji is Japan's highest mountain, standing at 3,776 meters (12,389 feet). Located on Honshu island about 100 km southwest of Tokyo, this active stratovolcano is renowned for its nearly perfect symmetrical cone, which remains snow-capped for approximately five months each year.
